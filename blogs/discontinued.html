Freakybob.site Has Been Discontinued.

The Freakybob Team has made the difficult decision to discontinue Freakybob.site. This is only discontinuing Freakybob.site and not the other projects. We will continue to pay to keep the website and subdomains online and well. When you visit Freakybob.site you'll see a pop-up message that reads:

"After almost 6 months of updates, we have finally reached the end. We are honestly burnt out of ideas for freakybob.site-- we have been since December-- but we stuck around. We're so grateful for the months of support and happiness that we have gained from this 1 website. We hope you understand about this decision and we're sorry for the inconvenience. As a thank you for everything, we have decided to make a gift for you! ".

As a thank you for your ongoing support, we’ve designed a unique background to show our gratitude. Generally thank you for everything!!!!!!!
